Black Widow

Their venom is 15 times as toxic as a prairie rattlesnake's, and they are not always black.

  • Scientific name: Latrodectus
  • Average length: 1.5 inches
  • Rank: Genus
  • Lifespan: 1-3 years

Black widow spiders are several species of arachnids in the genus Latrodectus that are known for the females' unique and striking appearance.
